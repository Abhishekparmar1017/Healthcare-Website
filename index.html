Product Categories

General Medicines

"General medicines" typically refer to medications that are commonly used to treat a wide range of common health conditions. These medicines are not specialized for any particular type of illness but are used in general medical practice to address various ailments.

Gastrointestinal Medicines

Gastrointestinalmedicines are medications used to treat various conditions and disorders of the digestive system, including the stomach, intestines, esophagus, liver, and pancreas. These medicines address issues such as acid reflux, ulcers, constipation, diarrhea, irritable bowel syndrome.

Gynaecological Medicines

Gynaecological medicines are used to treat various conditions and disorders related to the female reproductive system. These medications address issues such as menstrual disorders, hormonal imbalances, infections, infertility, and menopausal symptoms.
